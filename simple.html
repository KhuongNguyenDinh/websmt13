Movie 1: Inception

Dom Cobb is a skilled thief, the best in the dangerous art of extraction. He steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb's abilities have made him a coveted player in the world of corporate espionage, but they have also cost him everything he loves.

Movie 2: The Shawshank Redemption

Andy Dufresne, a successful banker, is sentenced to life imprisonment for the murder of his wife and her lover. In the prison, he forms a bond with a fellow inmate, Ellis "Red" Redding, and becomes instrumental in a money laundering operation.

Movie 3: The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

Frodo Baggins embarks on a perilous journey to destroy a powerful ring and prevent the Dark Lord Sauron from unleashing his evil forces upon the world. He is joined by a diverse group of companions as they form the Fellowship of the Ring and set out to Mount Doom.
